Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share information about the water rafting experience. This website will provide users with details, facts and other people's experiences doing water rafting, to help them prapare for their next adventure.

Audience

The target audience are males and females, ages from 12 to 50, that are about to embark in a white water rafting adventure for the first time, and are looking for tips, advices, and general information about water rafting.

Typography

Heading Font: Gooogle font: Abril Fatface

It is a stylish serif font yet still easy to read. I chose this fonts because the thin serifs and curves contrast with the paragaraph font.

Paragraph Font: Google font: Raleway

It is a sans serif font that will be easy to read. It helps paragraphs to look clean and not overwhelming.
