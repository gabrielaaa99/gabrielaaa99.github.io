Target Audience

The target audience of this website consists on women and men, ages from 18-60. They want to find nice products of good quality.
Target audience persona 1
Maria: 18 years, Medicine freshman student looking for her first uniform. She is looking for variety of uniform designs and colors. (Applies to any Health professional)
Our visitors wil find answers to questions about the products such as: prices, sizes, colors, availability and styles.
Visitors will also be able to complete activities in our website such as: ask questions, leave comments and watch images of products.
Visitors will be attracted to this site because it will be easy to use and understand, will have a simple and clean design, and will be organized.
