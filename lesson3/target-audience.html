Target Audience Description

The target audience for this site will be women and men, ages from 16 to 55 who need quick and easy to understand weather information alongside with clothing suggestions for the day.

Personas

Names: Andrea Riera and Peter Nava
Demographics: 21 years old, single, students.
Motivation: They take public transportation to go to college everyday. They would like to be prepared for the day with the right clothing, according to the weather.

Name: Claudia Martinez
Demographics: 50 years old, married with children, Landscape Architect, medium experience interacting with the web.
Motivation: She wants to be modern and comfortable to do daily tasks in spite of the weather. She will need quick and simple weather information to dress up fashion for job meetings.

Scenarios

I want to wear sandals today. Will this be a good day to do so?
This site will answer this kind of questions and provide appropiate clothing suggestions according to the weather.

What if I just want to see the weather for the day/week?
This site will primarily and always display Weather information, but it will also contain weather-based clothing suggestions.

Will the information be displayed in a weather map?
No, in this site weather maps won't be used.

Will I have to spend a lot of time reading/searching to see the weather?
No, this site's goal is to enhance user experience by presenting simple and clear weather information, that everyone can see and interpret with just a few clicks.

Will the clothing suggestions be personalized? Do I have to register/provide personal data?
No registration or personal data entry needed. The clothing suggestions will follow current fashion trends.
