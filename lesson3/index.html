The Weather Code.

Site Purpose

This site aims to provide users with accurate weather information over the Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center areas, along with clothing inspiration/suggestions based on the weather. The goal of this site is to offer users clear and veracious weather forecast and transform that information into style ideas that people can actually wear.
