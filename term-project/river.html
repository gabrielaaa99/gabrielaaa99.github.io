Be prepared to trust your guides and let go of a little control by getting to know some of them before your trip!

Meghan Clark, 27 — is one of our Kayaking experts. She's been sailing through rivers for almost 10 years. She is a full-time guide here at Riggins River Adventures. If you’ve ever been on the river with her, you’ll likely remember her contagious laugh!

Richard Perez, 30 — his love for the outdoors started as a child and only grew stronger when he started whitewater rafting Idaho rivers. With 10 years of experience, Richard loves sharing his knowledge with everyone that comes to Riggins River Adventures (specially those who come for the first time) and helping them to make the most of their experience!

Katheryne Marin, 25 — has over 5 years of experience and an adventurer spirit. She knows the rivers and how to sail them. Having her as your river guide guarantees lots of fun and the best advices!
